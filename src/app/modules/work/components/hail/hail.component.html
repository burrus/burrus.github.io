Hail

Hail was a photo-messaging app, inspired by Snapchat, built entirely in Objective-C using Parse as the back-end. The app is no longer available on the app store.

Hail was built in conjunction with my WWDC scholarship application. I applied for one of the WWDC scholarships through an organization known as the Thurgood Marshall College Fund back in 2015. I was lucky enough to have been selected for one of the scholarships and got to spend a week in San Francisco attending the conference.
